I started my college career like most young adults; not caring in the slightest about school or grades. I was living on my own for the first time and completely absorbed in the addictive freedoms of unsupervised adolescent life. I decided to follow my best friend to Gainesville, FL and attend school at Santa Fe Community College. I started working full time and going to school part time within about 6 months. A year and a half later, I switched my major for the fourth time and was sporting a 1.8 GPA. At some point I decided a change had to be made.
